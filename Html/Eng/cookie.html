1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files sent by the Website to the user's device, where they are stored and then retransmitted to the same websites upon subsequent visits.

They allow the user to be recognized and enhance the browsing experience.

2. Types of cookies we use

On our Website, we use:

  • Strictly necessary technical cookies: Essential for the proper functioning of the Website and to enable navigation and access to protected areas.
  • Preference cookies: Used to store information that alters the behavior or appearance of the Website, such as the preferred language.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to collect aggregated and anonymous data about the use of the Website, in order to improve its content and functionalities.

We currently do not use marketing cookies.
